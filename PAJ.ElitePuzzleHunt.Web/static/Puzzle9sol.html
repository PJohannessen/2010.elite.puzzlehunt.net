Color By Numbers (SOLUTION)
Author: QB
Difficulty: Hard

HINTS:
  1. Each name in the outer columns represents a feature film, though the rules for each column are different.
  2. Kate Hudson --[Matthew McConaughey]-- Kate Hudson 10
  3. The acting duos in the center column each have one film in common.
ANSWER: RAINBOWSIX

With a little research, each actor/actress in the outer columns can be paired with a film in which they had a large acting role. For actors in the left column, this is done by identifying the color of the actor's name and finding a film containing that color in its title. Similarly, the actors in the right column can be paired with their film titles that include one of the numbers given in the accompanying box. After discovering the films referenced in both outer columns, it's simply a matter of finding the actor who worked on BOTH films and filling him into the center column. The names in the center column are given in pairs, such that once you have a pair of names, you can find one and only one film that the two actors have worked on together. Taking the first letter of each of the 10 films gives the answer, RAINBOWSIX, a popular media franchise and upcoming film, the name of which consists of a spectrum of colors and another number.
