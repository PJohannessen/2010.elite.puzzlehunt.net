Gotta Solve 'Em All (SOLUTION)
Author: QB
Difficulty: Easy

HINTS:
  1. The numbers only went up to #151 originally.
  2. What's the difference between a Golem and a mole?
  3. The G!
ANSWER: CHANSEY

The title, based on Pokemon's popular catchphrase "Gotta Catch 'Em All", is your first clue that the numbers given refer to various Pokemon creatures. The clues beside each number can be solved with words that use all but one of the letters from that Pokemon's name. The extra letters, when read from top to bottom, provide yet another clue LAUGHINGANIMALS, which again can be solved with an answer one letter short of a Pokemon's name.
